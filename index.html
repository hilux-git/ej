EJ Presh & Clean

EJ Presh & Clean is a local pressure washing service devoted to a satisfying clean of patios, pavement, driveways, concrete, etc. Run by two local high school students, EJ Presh & Clean has exemplary experience in the field of pressure washing and drive-top/patio cleaning. We finish our pressure washing with a seal coat to seal your driveway, and add longevity to the clean look after the wash.




Where do we offer our services?

EJ Presh & Clean offers service to people residing in Bonney Lake, Buckley and Enumclaw.


What does a full service include?

A full service includes a pressure wash of the surface being cleaned, and following a complete pressure wash, the surface is coated with a sealing top coat to preserve the surface top, and keep it cleaner for longer.


How much do we charge?

We do not offer set prices per square foot as square footage doesn't take into account complexity of the area. Instead, we offer an affordable quote upon contact.
